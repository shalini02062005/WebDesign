XHTML Templates

XHTML templates are templates that use Extensible HyperText Markup Language (XHTML), a more rigid and XML-based version of HTML. XHTML is a cross between HTML and XML, developed by the World Wide Consortium (W3C) in 2000. It's designed to work with XML-based applications and can be viewed, edited, and validated with standard XML tools.
